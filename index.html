The impossible Game

The French railway company SNCF was looking to recruit engineers able to imagine the future of mobility. The idea was to build a recruiting platform around ultra-realistic futuristic scenarios that will test young engineers by asking them to find solutions to the upcoming challenges facing SNCF and thus identify the precious few who have what it takes to engineer the future of the transportation

Developed in HTML 5 Canvas, the SNCF Engineering Challenge boasts a stunning illustrative style and a library of design elements that create a visually exciting futuristic world through which players navigate with their desktop keyboards or using their smartphones as mobile controllers for a more immersive gaming experience.

LE LABO FRANGACE

Born in Grasse and raised in New York where Le Labo’s first boutique was opened in 2006, the brand relies on its craftwomen and craftmen, the lab technician, the candle pourer, the rose picker among others, to shape and contribute to its world. Some of Le Labo locations are designed as a fragrance lab open to public where visitors have the opportunity to awaken their sense of smell to different fragrances and the raw materials used in

The “cult of imperfection" guides Le Labo’s creative spirit leading to th eir delicately chosen and beautifully structured scents. The creative process adheres to the Japanese world view of wabi-sabi, described as “the beauty of imperfection,  the awareness of the impermanence of things. It is more of a feeling, a visual and almost spiritual impression, than a rational concept. That is the quiet beauty they try to achieve in their perfumes.

THE INVICTUS AWARD SEASOM ll

Paco Rabanne dreamed up the Invictus Award to promote its sport-themed perfume. We supplied the buzz. We implemented an unprecedented digital experience focused on the Invictus Award, bringing together the major communication tools currently available on the web: an awarded website, a Custom YouTube channel and a Facebook page. For each one of the two seasons,

over 20 episodes were posted to the site continually, revealing the athletes who were placed at the center of a state-of-the-art platform. A real battle was struck between the athletes to get internet users votes. The results : over 1M unique visitors on the platform, 40M video views on the webshow and 200 000 votes on the platform for both Season 01 & Season 02.
